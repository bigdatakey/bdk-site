Features

Who owns the information, he owns the world. Modern business is unthinkable without managing all the processes in the company and interacting with customers online. BDK CRM is a system that offers you many convenient tools for this.

Task manager
You or someone from your employees can, in a few clicks, distribute tasks to others and monitor the progress of their completion.
Customer interaction
Using BDK CRM, you can communicate with your customers, receive requests from them, issue invoices and exchange documents.
IP-telephony and office PBX
Together with BDK CRM, you will have access to call-forwarding settings, creating leads based on incoming calls, free calls between employees and other functions.
Running the leads
Creating a list of potential customers with all the necessary accompanying information. Ability to redeploy leads into active client status
Reporting and documentation
Drawing up reports and estimates, storage of contracts and knowledge base - all this is included in the BDK CRM.
Internet acquiring
Your customers will be able to pay for your services online with bankcards.

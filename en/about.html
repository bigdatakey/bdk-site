Our Company

Big Data Key is a Russian company focused on the domestic and international information technology market. Among our clients are companies from Russia, the UK, Germany, Australia and Israel.

Big Data Key works in the following areas of IT-services:

  • •   web development and creation of mobile applications,
  • •   promotion and advertising on the Internet,
  • •   big data and machine learning,
  • •   automation for business.

Several experts, whose experience in the IT-industry is more than 10 years, founded big Data Key. Now the company has o staff of more than 35 people.

Backend and frontend developers, big data programmers, designers, illustrators, internet marketers, BI analysts and managers with different specializations represent the backbone of the Big Data Key.

Our company is not just a commercial enterprise for creating and promoting software. Big Data Key is a way of asserting our values and bringing our ideals into reality.

The first value of Big Data Key is interaction.

We work with our clients as a team taking all their needs as our own. Practice shows that such involvement in the affairs of the client - it is not a trivial and difficult task but the only way achieve the best result.

The second value of Big Data Key is people.

We believe that respectful and humane attitude towards our customers and each other should be the hallmark of our company. Becoming part of Big Data Key means not only meeting our professional requirements but also being spiritually close to us.

The third value of Big Data Key is technology.

We fanatically monitor the development of our industry, use the best ideas and contribute to the technological progress if it is possible. Leadership in technology provides global leadership. This rule is true for us and for our customers.
